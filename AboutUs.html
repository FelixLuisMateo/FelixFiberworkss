About Felix Fiberworks

Welcome to Felix Fiberworks—a joyful world of handmade fiber art! Meet our team, discover our story, and see what makes our brand truly special.

Our Story

Felix Fiberworks began with a skein of yarn and a dream: to bring color and comfort to the world, one handmade creation at a time.

What started as a hobby in a cozy corner has blossomed into a vibrant studio. Every plushie, flower, and embroidery piece is crafted with care, heart, and a dash of magic.

We believe that handcrafted art connects us—to tradition, to the planet, and to each other. Our products are inspired by nature, childhood joy, and the desire to make every day a little brighter.
